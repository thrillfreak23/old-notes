examples

- hekehâhênâleb rûthisot :: Rain falls stemming from the Way of Peace.

- hokekâl Kak kenetir rûthisot :: Kakon hit the dog seeking out peace.

- sharkekâl Kak mûmburnot kenetethid vêgorir :: Kak hit the wolf to
  save his dog from death.

- sorfîshikinazâl Kak thinskînenot :: Kak is "terminaling" in
  mental/inward pain.
