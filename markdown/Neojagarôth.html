NEOJAGARÔTH

  • Jagarôth with thematic voice system

Outer Structure summary

  • Comparative system
    • OUT- (exceed)
    • UNDER- (fall short)
  • Transfer system
    • DAT- (recipient core)
    • FOR- (benefactive)
    • FROM- (source)
  • Social/relational system
    • WITH- (Comitative)
    • AGAINST- (oppositional)
    • OVER- (dominance)
  • Cognitive/internal system
    • IN- (internalization)

Design constraints

  • Prefixes must belong to exactly one domain
    • comparative
    • transfer
    • social
    • cognitive
  • Only one (1) domain may control core argument structure at a time

fully worked sentence paradigm

  • Neutral baseline Kak.NOM give money.ACC church.DAT Tam.OBL dregâl Kak yurot kirkôdh Tamist Kak gives money to the church, Tam is just there. ==> comparison implied pragmatically

  • WITH- frame

    • (comitative voice)
    • (co-participant emphasis)
