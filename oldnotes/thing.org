** fon :: nominal :: light in the visible spectrum
*** kefet is the more scientific term for light but is sometimes used in everyday speech
** lay :: verbal :: to radiate
** te :: preposition :: introduces a dependent clause inside a larger utteranance
*** example :: pê spelâlet te Obar î lûth :: he said that the Emperor is good
** bûbur :: nominal :: mouth
** thesh :: nominal :: wind
** hûdh :: verbal :: to shit
*** hûdha :: adjective :: shitty
** jed :: perfective aspectual :: denotes completed, finished actions
*** jed kwîzalen "I already pissed"
** -thor :: clitic :: and, the word-level conjunctive enclitic
*** does not join clauses :: koy is the word for "and" that joins clauses
*** -thor can join together adjectivals, nominals, verbals, etc, but to join two clauses you use koy
