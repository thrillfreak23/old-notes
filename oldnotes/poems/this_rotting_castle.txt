`
this rotting castle 
    stuffed with fallen ragged pompous kings

this sensorium melting around the edges
    lips kissing the poison in a spoon

these irrumations of the mind 
    flapping rushing slapping past
    
downpayments on damnation
donations to dispossession
    deposits of shame sunken into bedrock
    scream past like crows
    cut like knaps of neanderthal flint
`
